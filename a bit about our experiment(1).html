Welcome to the TripTip website! Israel's attractions site

Here is a series of web pages, with one or several questions on each page.

The following questionnaire will express your preferences regarding different

types of attractions (you will be required to rank on a moving scale),

so that the system can perform an adjustment calculation.

Once your preferences are loaded, the system will show you its recommendation.

To complete the experiment you must answer the questions until you reach the end page.

It is not possible to go back between pages. Therefore, move forward only when

the answers you have provided are final for you.

The experiment should be performed alone and independently.
You are asked not to pass on information about the content of the experiment to any other participant.
